About

Magna Lux Foundation

We are committed to raising a generation built on God's Word and driven by purpose. Through intentional discipleship, we equip young people with biblical truth and essential skills

You may support a little by contactacting us. Thank you.

OUR MISSION:

To enhance our generation to become the light of the world. We root them in God's Word and empower them through capacity building and skill development.

OUR VISION:

To spread God's Word globally, revealing Christ as the true Light. We equip youth with godly character and practical skills to become vessels of transformation.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Discipleship Through God's Word
Disciple young people through sound teaching of God's Word.

Spiritual Events
Organize biblical seminars, youth revivals, and spirit-filled workshops in schools.

Practical Equipping
Equip students with practical and digital skils to live purposefully for Christ.

Mentorship & Support
Offer Godly counsel, career mentorship, and emotional support to students.

OUR TARGET GROUPS

Junior High School Students

We reach students through Bible-centered teachings and introductory skill training. Monthly fellowship gatherings include worship and Word studies.

Senior High School Students

SHS students establish Word-based fellowships and discipleship groups. We support them with visits, resources, and special events.

Tertiary Students

We continue discipleship with college-age students, preparing them for leadership in their fields.

Outreach Focus

We extend our ministry to orphanages and people on the streets, sharing God’s love.
